Global Water Futures (GWF) data Cuizinart

email: gwf.cuizinart@uwaterloo.ca

GWF aims to position Canada as a global leader in water science for cold regions and will address the strategic needs of the Canadian economy in adapting to change and managing risks of uncertain water futures and extreme events. The GWF-Cuizinart to crop and subset geo-referenced data is developed at the University of Waterloo by Jimmy Lin (https://cs.uwaterloo.ca/~jimmylin/) and Juliane Mai (http://www.civil.uwaterloo.ca/jmai/).
